Story

Cammie Chameleon wakes up in a cage in a cave! In her attempt to escape this predicament she discovers she must figure out how to manipulate beams of light in order to activate the crystal switches that lead to the next chamber of the cave. Will Cammie Chameleon be able to solve the light puzzles in order to find freedom?

Instructions

Use the arrow keys to move Cammie Chameleon. Move the components in the room to hit the crystals with color-matching beams. Once all the crystals in the room glow, the exit is unblocked!

Press 'r' or right "ctrl" key to restart the level.

To mix colors, make the light beams intersect each other. Look at the color Venn Diagram for how to get the color you want.

Note: You may not need to use all the parts in the room to solve the puzzle. You also do not need to bring a component from the previously-solved room into the new room.

Movable Components

If it's not the right angle, it's the wrong angle.

ImageDescriptionExample Diagram(s)
Double-sided Mirror:
Reflects light beams at 90 degree angles on both sides. It also reflects the light beam back in the opposite direction if light hits perpendicularly to the mirror.
One-way Mirror:
Reflects light beams at 90 degree angles on one side and passes light beam directly through from the other side.
Refractor Mirror:
Reflects light at 45 degrees angle.
Collimator:
Combines the light beam from any direction and directs one combined beam of light.
Splitter:
Splits the light beam to left and right sides of object (in a T-shape) if hit at the triangular point.

Filters:

Change white beam to the filter’s color.

Sometimes filters will be movable and sometimes they are not, be sure to test it out!

Unmovable Components

ImageDescription
Prism:
Splits up white beam into red (parallel to white light), green (at a 45 degree angle), and blue beams (perpendicular to white light). The prisms are stuck in stalagmite, so they are unmovable.

Crystals:
Make all of the crystals in the room glow to open the exit.
Black Orbs:
Blocks the room exit. Will disappear when all the crystals are lit.
Beam shooter:
Shoots beams of white light or different color lights.
